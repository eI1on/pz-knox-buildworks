title=[B42] Knox Buildworks
description=[h1]Knox Buildworks[/h1]
description=[i]Plan it. See it. Build it.[/i]
description=
description=Knox Buildworks is a complete, independent construction experience for [b]B42[/b]: a searchable building catalogue, clearer recipe requirements, multi-tile placement, wall finishing, persistent blueprints, shared planning tools, and server-authoritative multiplayer validation.
description=
description=It coexists with the vanilla Building Menu instead of replacing its entity scripts. Build normally, design an entire project first, or create JSON-powered add-ons without patching the base UI.
description=
description=[hr][/hr]
description=[h2]At a glance[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*][b]Modern construction catalogue[/b] with search, filters, sorting, categories, favourites, recent entries, grouped stages, variants and material sets.
description=[*][b]Requirements you can understand[/b], including exact items, accepted alternatives, tools, uses, skills, learned recipes, light and construction time.
description=[*][b]Full Planning Mode[/b] with rooms, levels, ghost buildables, gather areas, totals, access permissions and queued construction.
description=[*][b]Real multi-tile geometry[/b] for directional, layered, stacked and irregular objects-not a collection of fake 1x1 previews.
description=[*][b]Wall finishing pipeline[/b] for plaster, paint, wallpaper and sign variants, with the correct tools, selected materials and final sprites.
description=[*][b]Multiplayer-first safety[/b] with definition integrity checks, permission-aware blueprint sync and server-side build validation.
description=[*][b]Data-driven add-on framework[/b] built around manifests, reusable templates and clean JSON definitions.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[quote]
description=[b]Quick start[/b]
description=Press [b]F7[/b] to open Knox Buildworks, or use the Building Menu drawer on the left side of the screen. Open [b]Plans[/b] to enter Planning Mode.
description=
description=A sandbox option can make Knox Buildworks the primary Building Menu action. Otherwise, Knox and the vanilla menu remain available side by side.
description=[/quote]
description=
description=[h2]A catalogue built for large construction sets[/h2]
description=Browse converted Build 42 construction through a dedicated interface covering walls, frames, floors, doors, windows, stairs, fences, gates, barriers, furniture, agriculture, animal structures, blacksmithing, pottery, outdoor objects and wall finishes.
description=
description=[list]
description=[*]Switch between a visual grid and a detailed list.
description=[*]Search by recipe name or required item.
description=[*]Filter by category, subcategory, material and skill.
description=[*]Sort alphabetically while keeping pinned and favourite recipes easy to reach.
description=[*]Choose stages from a carousel instead of browsing duplicate Level 1, Level 2 and Level 3 entries.
description=[*]Select variants, material sets and finishes only when the buildable actually provides them.
description=[*]Preview directional and multi-tile footprints with their real sprites and dimensions.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Requirements that explain themselves[/h2]
description=Knox separates [b]Materials & Tools[/b] from [b]Skills & Knowledge[/b], then explains how every requirement participates in the recipe.
description=
description=[list]
description=[*]See whether an item is [b]consumed[/b], [b]kept[/b], [b]drained by uses[/b], selected from alternatives, or may degrade during construction.
description=[*]Click a requirement to open the integrated [b]Available Ingredients / Possible Items[/b] drawer.
description=[*]Available Ingredients shows the real matching inventory, grouped by item type and expandable to individual stacks.
description=[*]Possible Items shows every exact item or Build 42 item tag accepted by the recipe.
description=[*]Choose which valid alternative should be consumed; the choice is remembered and validated again before building.
description=[*]Review skill levels, magazine/recipe knowledge, XP, real-time duration, required light, hand props and input rules before placement.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Build, rotate and finish[/h2]
description=The Knox cursor uses Build 42 construction rules for supports, floors, wall edges, previous stages, door and window frames, stairs, vehicles, collisions, stackable furniture and multi-tile geometry.
description=
description=[list]
description=[*]Construction uses timed actions, recipe animations, sounds and hand models where the imported entity data provides them.
description=[*]Native-style tool degradation flags are respected for kept tools.
description=[*]Continue placing compatible objects while materials remain available.
description=[*]Build walls bare, plastered, plastered and painted, or plastered and wallpapered.
description=[*]Finish requirements remain exact: plastering trowel and plaster; paintbrush and the selected paint; or wallpaper, paste, paintbrush and scissors.
description=[*]Standalone plaster, paint, wallpaper and sign actions can target compatible existing walls.
description=[*]Add-on tile packs can register their own wall surfaces and finish sprite mappings.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Knox Planning Mode[/h2]
description=Design a project before committing materials. Planning Mode is a separate workspace with blueprint management and room tools on the left, the world in the centre, and a purpose-built planning catalogue on the right.
description=
description=[list]
description=[*]Create, rename, duplicate, move and delete independent blueprints.
description=[*]Plan on another Z level without moving your character there.
description=[*]Place persistent, non-blocking ghost buildables with their selected direction, stage, variant, material and finish.
description=[*]Draw walls in lines and floors in rectangles. Large, irregular and multi-tile objects remain single placements to avoid broken repetition.
description=[*]Plan compatible floors, walls, finishes and furniture on the same tile without flattening them into one layer.
description=[*]Draw named, coloured rooms and edit them independently from planned construction.
description=[*]Define a gather area that can include world containers and vehicle storage.
description=[*]Review total materials, tools, skills, learned recipes, rooms and planned objects.
description=[*]Build one ghost, use the world build tool, or queue a complete blueprint. Completed plans disappear only after their matching world object is created.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Blueprint sharing and HUD tracking[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*]Blueprints persist with the save or server.
description=[*]Export plans as origin-relative JSON, copy their JSON, or write them onto a Knox Blueprint item made from paper.
description=[*]Import a blueprint and choose a new world origin before construction.
description=[*]Pin recipes or complete blueprint totals to the HUD with icons or text, status colours, alignment, opacity, accent-bar and manual-position options.
description=[*]Blueprint owners can configure default, faction and per-player [b]view[/b], [b]build[/b] and [b]contribute[/b] permissions.
description=[*]Explicit player permissions override faction and default access. Only owners and administrators can manage access.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Multiplayer integrity and compatibility[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*]The server and client calculate a deterministic identity from participating definition files and save-specific overrides. Mismatched clients cannot use Knox construction or mutate plans.
description=[*]Every build request is checked server-side: buildable, stage, variant, material, ingredient choices, tools, consumables, skills, recipes, finish, distance, Z level, permissions, placement and collisions.
description=[*]Blueprints are synchronized only to players allowed to view them and relevant to their current area; routine edits use small deltas instead of resending the whole plan.
description=[*]Definitions are registered by unique ID rather than sprite. Shared sprites are allowed inside Knox without registering duplicate vanilla entity scripts.
description=[*]Malformed add-on definitions are logged and skipped when safe instead of crashing the complete registry.
description=[*]Client definition loading is spread across frames to reduce loading and joining spikes.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Options[/h2]
description=[b]Sandbox options[/b]
description=[list]
description=[*]Use Knox Buildworks as the primary Building Menu action.
description=[*]Enable or disable Planning Mode.
description=[*]Configure blueprint radius and maximum placement count.
description=[*]Configure the construction XP multiplier.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[b]Player options[/b]
description=[list]
description=[*]Change the F7 keybind.
description=[*]Configure pinned-recipe position, alignment, opacity, accent bar and icon/text presentation.
description=[*]Enable additional debug logging for definition and UI development.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]For add-on authors[/h2]
description=Knox Buildworks is also a modular content framework. Standard add-ons can provide a manifest and JSON definitions without broad UI patches or duplicate entity-script registration.
description=
description=[list]
description=[*]Reusable templates and material groups.
description=[*]Grouped stages, variants and material options.
description=[*]Directional sprite patterns and optional custom icons.
description=[*]Layered 3D matrices for multi-tile and irregular footprints.
description=[*]Exact items, Build 42 tags, drainable uses, tool flags, skills and learned recipes.
description=[*]Wall surfaces, plaster, paint, wallpaper and custom finish mappings.
description=[*]Safe entity compatibility metadata and focused Lua extension APIs.
description=[*]Per-save/server definition overrides and integrity-aware multiplayer loading.
description=[/list]
description=
description=Full player, server-owner and add-on documentation is available in the project documentation site and repository.
